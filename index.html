About Me

17 years old. Getting it done.

I noticed a problem. Small businesses — restaurants, food trucks, local spots — had amazing food but nothing online to back it up. No website, no presence, nothing. Customers couldn't find them, and they were losing business because of it.

So I started building websites for them. Luxury websites at a not-so-luxury price. Every site I make is custom, mobile-ready, bilingual if needed, and built to actually represent the business properly.

I'm 17, I go to Monte Vista High School, and I work at Roundhill Country Club. I'm also sponsored by PHYR Pickleball and do sales for LuxWash Co. I stay busy — and I bring that same work ethic to every site I build.
